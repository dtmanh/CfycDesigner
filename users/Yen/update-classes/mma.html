frequently asked questions

Why do I need to register online and what will happen?

We will contact you within 24 hours to schedule an appointment for you at your nearest California Fitness & Yoga Centers and ensure that an MMA / Kickfit instructor is available to meet you during your first visit to introduce you to the program and help you prepare.

What exactly will I receive?

You’ll receive a full one hour, 1 on 1 introductory kick fit / MMA session with a kickfit instructor along with a personalized fitness consultation with a personal trainer and 5 days of full, unlimited access to all California Fitness and Yoga facilities including weights, equipment, sauna, steam room etc.

Are the benefits of Kickfit and MMA?

Kick fit and MMA are great alternatives if you are looking for a more fast paced and exciting exercise which is effective in burning fat, toning muscle and also developing strength and cardio / conditioning.

What will happen during the personalized fitness consultation?

A personal trainer will meet with you to help you learn more about your body, how you can reach your goals and understand what types of exercise and nutrition is most suitable for you and your needs.

What is MMA / Kickfit like? Is it safe?

MMA and Kickfit are fun, exciting and high energy ways to get fit quickly. You’ll spend time in an international standard MMA ring with your trainer practicing very safe and fast paced fighting moves. All equipment including pads, gloves etc. will be provided for your first time.
